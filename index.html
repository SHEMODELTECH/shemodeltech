About

A non-profit dedicated to educating and mentoring women who want to start or advance their careers in information technology

Get Started Watch Video

Vision

To increase the representation of women from underserved communities in the field of information technology.

What we have for you

Mentorship and IT training

Eligibility

Women interested in developing a career in information technology.

Why you need to join SHE MODEL TECH

  • Gain access to quality, free IT resources and skills that are relevant to your area of expertise.
  • Get connected to people developing a career in your tech field.
  • Get help and clarity when stuck in your tech field.
  • Get a career guide and clarity on how to start a career in tech.
  • Get free and simple access to mentors in your field of technology from different backgrounds and around the world.
